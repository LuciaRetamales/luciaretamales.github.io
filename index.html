Shortly About Me

I’m Lucia Retamales, an animator from Argentina. I bring narratives, advertisements and indie projects to motion! I have vast experience in all the steps of producing an animated video. From storyboarding and character animation to composition and animated graphics.

As a freelancer, I do cutscenes and promotions of comics, games, books and more. As an art director, I lead my very capable team towards doing narrative-driven advertisements (2D and 3D), for clients such as Merge Mansion and Angry Birds Journey. I’m also looking forward doing 3D indie short films with my team in the near future.
